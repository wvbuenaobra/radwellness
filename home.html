RADify Wellness is a team of young professionals - counselors, psychologists, human resource and business professionals - who are advocates of mental health. Through our years in the industry, we have gained expertise in the corporate, educational and community sectors and have strengthened our passion to help groups and individuals through various mental health intervention and programs.
