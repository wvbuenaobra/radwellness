WHO WE ARE

Angel Aniag

Angel, as the Chief Mental Health Professional of RAD, is a Registered Guidance Counselor and a board placer of the 2012 licensure exam of the Professional Regulations Commission (PRC) for Guidance Counselors. He has completed his Master’s Degree at the De La Salle University-Dasmarinas, magna cum laude.

His professional career revolved around identifying learning needs, designing programs, facilitating training sessions, and evaluation for 10 years working in corporate companies and educational institutions. He has received his Development Dimensions International (DDI) Certification in facilitation of globally trusted leadership development programs in 2014. He has also served clientele in the field of counseling handling psychological issues and concerns.

His work as a mental health professional and advocate has been devoted to the integration of psychological and mental health programs in business and corporate environment through customized psychoeducational training programs and counseling services via traditional and innovative platforms.

Doreen Flores

Doreen finished her Bachelors Degree in Psychology at the University of Santo Tomas. Presently, she is taking up her Master’s Degree in Counseling Psychology where she had gained significant experience in Psychological research, group therapy for heterogenous groups, and individual counseling helping young adults and adolescents. She is the Lead Health Care Provider of RAD.

Her 12 years of broad expertise in the human resource function ranges from talent acquisition, career and performance management, employee engagement, compensation and benefits in educational and banking industry. In the field of counseling, she had handled cases of clients dealing with various concerns and life problems such as poverty, dislocation, depression, marital and relationships, bullying, and issues of adolescents.

Her work as a mental health professional focused on the holistic wellness of people in all kinds of work settings or living environments through effective and customized psychoeducational and counseling services via traditional and innovative approaches.

WHAT WE DO

We design and implement complete wellness programs that include various methods such as seminars, workshops, and professional counseling.

These programs give importance to the needs, preferences, and availability of the clients. We also ensure that these programs are preventive, developmental, and remedial in nature to guarantee that we are holistic in our approach. We all make these possible by merging traditional and technology-driven methodologies in our interventions such as online counseling, application-based programs, and chatbots.
