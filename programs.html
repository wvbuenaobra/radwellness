Training Programs

1. In-house Programs

Aligned to RAD’s objective of bringing mental health awareness to different organizations and companies, our In-house programs offer psychoeducational learning experiences that is customized to the needs of the employees, which aims to educate and equip the organization for a more resilient and productive workforce.

This type of program is designed for organizations and companies that:

  • Are looking for customized mental health learning programs for their employees
  • Prefer their training programs done in their chosen venue, location, schedule, and budget

2. Public Seminars

RAD’s ultimate goal is to reach each and every individual and educate them on mental wellness. Our public learning offerings aim to offer quality learning experiences that would focus on relevant topics on mental health and equip individuals on skills that are practical and useful.

This type of program is recommended for:

  • Individual who is either from an organization/company or not, who is interested to take our learning program
  • Organizations / companies who have 1 to 10 learners who would want to attend a learning program
  • Individuals who would like to meet other learners
  • Organizations / companies who would like a more convenient way of training their employees because we take care of everything: venue, food, and all logistical requirements

Psychological Interventions

1. Group Counseling

RAD has a pool of professional counselors and health care providers who had the expertise to facilitate group counseling sessions for individuals whether coming from one organization/company or from a diverse background.

2. Individual Counseling

In addition to RAD’s expertise of offering preventive psychoeducational learning sessions, the team has gained expertise in conducting counseling services both one-on-one counseling and online platforms. This is to ensure to meet the preference, availability, and needs of the client.

Employee Wellness Program

RAD offers a complete wellness program for an organization that aims to promote and create a mentally well and healthy workforce. Programs may include learning sessions, application-based programs, chatbot and real-person healthcare providers, workshops, exercises and the like.
